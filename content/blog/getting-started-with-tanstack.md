There's a reason the phrase "fits like a glove" is the highest compliment in menswear. No matter how luxurious the fabric or how impeccable the design, a suit that doesn't fit properly will never look its best.

## Off-the-Rack vs. Made-to-Measure vs. Bespoke

**Off-the-rack** suits are produced in standard sizes. They're convenient and accessible, but they're designed for an average body — and no one is truly average.

**Made-to-measure** starts with an existing pattern that's adjusted to your measurements. It's a step up, offering a better fit at a moderate price point.

**Bespoke** is where true craftsmanship lives. A bespoke suit is built from scratch — a unique pattern created for your body alone. Every element, from shoulder width to trouser break, is considered and refined through multiple fittings.

## Why Fit Matters More Than Brand

A well-fitted suit from a skilled tailor will always outperform an ill-fitting designer label. Fit communicates attention to detail, self-awareness, and professionalism. It's the difference between wearing a suit and owning the room.
